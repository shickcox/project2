* How they are helpful *

When you are feeling down they can sense that something is wrong and the try their best to comfort you.

They can sense danger and they alert you by barking, they even try to attack in order to protect you.

In police units they use dog's to help find missing or lost people and they can also search for items a person may have in their possession.

They can even keep you active.

Lastly Dog's can also be used as guide dog's which help people within their everyday lives ; for people who are blind ; people who have mental issues ; and finally people who have health issues even.
